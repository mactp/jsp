Friends of Jacob Smith Park

The Friends of JSP are a community group dedicated to preserving and enhancing our beautiful park for everyone to enjoy.

Who Are We?

The Friends of Jacob Smith Park (FoJSP) is a Community Interest Group formed at the suggestion of the council in 2008, shortly after JSP opened to the public.

We work closely with North Yorkshire Council (NYC), who own the park and are responsible for its upkeep, to agree priorities and help deliver the yearly plan.

What We Do

  • Represent the interests of the local community and park users.
  • Fundraise for improvements outside the council’s responsibility.
  • Arrange volunteer events to enhance the park.
  • Hold public meetings twice a year to share progress and gather views.
  • Ensure all work meets the wishes set out in Miss Jacob Smith’s will.

Read more: FoJSP Constitution, Minutes of last public meeting, Minutes of last AGM.

Recent Projects

Benches

Funded and installed seven oak benches for resting and enjoying the park views.

Bat & Bird Boxes

Installed a variety of boxes for species including owls, robins, nuthatches, and bats.

Tree Planting

Planted over 300 trees to expand woodland habitat.

Wildflowers

Cleared areas and planted hundreds of native wildflowers to support pollinators.
