About the Friends of Jacob Smith Park

We are a group of volunteers who care deeply about preserving, protecting and celebrating Jacob Smith Park.

Who We Are

The Friends of Jacob Smith Park is a volunteer-led group formed to support the park’s upkeep, raise awareness of its natural and historical value, and ensure it continues to be a space enjoyed by everyone.

What We Do

  • Organise community clean-ups, planting, and maintenance days
  • Support biodiversity and conservation projects
  • Host public events and guided walks
  • Work with local schools and groups to provide learning opportunities
  • Engage with local councils to ensure the park’s long-term protection

Current Projects

  • Wildflower Restoration: Reintroducing native species to boost biodiversity
  • Accessible Pathways: Improving access for wheelchairs and pushchairs
  • Story Trail: A fun, educational trail for children and families (launching Summer 2025)

How You Can Help

We welcome new members and volunteers! Whether you can give time, expertise or ideas, there's a place for you in the Friends group.

  • Become a member
