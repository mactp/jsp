Support the Park

Your donation helps protect, maintain, and enhance Jacob Smith Park for future generations.

Why Donate?

Jacob Smith Park is a vital green space for people and wildlife in Knaresborough. As a community-supported space, donations are essential to:

  • Preserve and restore wildlife habitats
  • Maintain paths, benches, and facilities
  • Provide educational signage and events
  • Fund volunteer projects and conservation work

Even small contributions go a long way in helping us care for this precious space.

How to Donate
