Support the Park

Your donation helps protect, maintain, and enhance Jacob Smith Park for future generations of people and wildlife.

Why Your Support Matters

Jacob Smith Park is a cherished green space at the heart of Knaresborough. With no entrance fees, we rely on the generosity of people like you to keep it thriving. Your donations help us to:

  • Preserve and restore wildlife habitats
  • Maintain paths, benches, and visitor facilities
  • Provide educational signage and events
  • Fund volunteer projects and conservation work

Even a small gift goes a long way in helping us care for this precious place.
