About Jacob Smith Park

Jacob Smith Park (JSP) is a 30‑acre green space in Scriven, close to the centre of Knaresborough. It was generously bequeathed to the community by Winifred Jacob Smith MBE, following her death in 2003.

It was Miss Jacob Smith’s wish that the park should be opened for all generations to enjoy “the freedom and beauty that public parks bring” and she specified a few restrictions to limit the development of the park and keep it a natural space.

JSP is a managed wild space with a wide variety of habitats and wildlife to enjoy as you walk around. It is a tranquil space to spend time with friends or to simply relax on a bench and enjoy the peaceful surroundings. There is also an open space where children can enjoy playing ball games, flying a kite or running around.

Whatever the reason for your visit, we hope you enjoy this lovely green space as Miss Jacob Smith wished.
