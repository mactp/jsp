Planning Your Visit

Whether it's your first time or your hundredth, here's everything you need to enjoy your time at Jacob Smith Park.

What to Enjoy

  • Nature Trails: Discover winding paths through woodland and wildflower meadows.
  • Wildlife Watching: Visit our Wildlife page to learn what to look for.
  • Picnic Spots: Enjoy peaceful moments with friends or family.
  • History Walks: Learn more about our fascinating past on the History page.
  • Community Events: Check out what's on or get involved yourself.

Respecting the Park and Each Other

We want the park to be a safe, inclusive, and clean environment for everyone. Please:

  • Keep dogs on a lead in sensitive areas, and always pick up after them.
  • Stick to marked paths to protect wildlife and habitats.
  • Dispose of litter responsibly or take it home with you.
  • Be mindful of noise levels—this is a space for tranquility and reflection.
  • Respect other visitors, especially during busy periods.

Want to do more? Learn how you can support the park by getting involved or making a donation.
